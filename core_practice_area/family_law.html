Family Law

At our firm, we understand that family law matters often come with deeply emotional challenges. Divorce, child custody disputes, and issues surrounding child or spousal support can significantly impact your life and well-being. Whether you are going through a divorce, dealing with custody arrangements, or facing difficulties related to financial support, we provide compassionate and effective legal guidance to help you through tough times.

Family law is a specialized area of law that deals with legal issues related to family relationships, including marriage, divorce, child custody, adoption, spousal and child support, and more. Our experienced family law attorneys are dedicated to protecting your rights and advocating for a fair resolution, while also ensuring the best possible outcome for you and your loved ones.

Divorce and Separation

Divorce can be one of the most difficult events in a person’s life, both emotionally and financially. Whether the divorce is contested or uncontested, we are here to provide you with the legal support and guidance needed to navigate the process. Our attorneys will ensure that your interests are protected, whether it involves the division of assets, child custody arrangements, or spousal support.

We help our clients understand the legal procedures involved in divorce and work with them to achieve a resolution that minimizes conflict and stress. Our team offers compassionate advice on how to approach difficult conversations, protect your assets, and navigate complex legal issues. For more details on how to prepare for a divorce, visit our Divorce Guide.

Child Custody and Parenting Time

Child custody is often the most emotionally charged aspect of divorce proceedings. We recognize the importance of ensuring that your child's well-being is prioritized and that you have the necessary legal support during this challenging time. Whether you are seeking custody or are involved in a dispute regarding parenting time, we can help you navigate the legal process and advocate for a solution that is in the best interest of your child.

Our firm handles both physical custody and legal custody cases, helping parents understand the various custody arrangements and what factors courts typically consider when determining custody decisions. We are here to guide you through the process of creating a parenting plan that works for both you and your child. Learn more about child custody by visiting our Child Custody Guide.

Child and Spousal Support

Child and spousal support can be a crucial part of divorce or separation proceedings. If you are a parent seeking child support or if you are seeking or responding to spousal support claims, our attorneys can help you understand your rights and obligations under the law.

We work with clients to ensure that the correct amount of support is determined based on the guidelines provided by state law, taking into account factors such as income, financial needs, and the best interests of the child. Whether you are seeking to modify an existing support order or need help with a new support arrangement, we are here to provide sound legal advice. For more information, visit our Child Support page.

In cases where support amounts are not being paid as ordered or the paying party is experiencing financial difficulties, we also provide assistance with enforcement and modification of support orders. We ensure that our clients' financial needs are met and that support obligations are upheld. Visit our Spousal Support page for more details on spousal support issues.

Alimony and Spousal Support

Spousal support, also known as alimony, is often a contentious issue in divorce cases. Our attorneys help ensure that fair and reasonable spousal support agreements are reached, taking into account both parties’ financial needs, earning capacities, and other relevant factors.

If you are entitled to spousal support, we will help you understand how it is calculated, and if you are obligated to pay support, we will help you ensure that the terms of the agreement are fair and in accordance with the law. For more on alimony, visit our Alimony Guide.

Property Division

Dividing assets during a divorce can be a complex process. Our family law attorneys are skilled in handling the division of marital property, ensuring that your interests are protected and that assets are divided fairly under the law. We work with clients to ensure that they understand the nature of the assets involved, whether they are real property, financial accounts, or personal property.

Property division also includes dealing with the family home, investments, retirement funds, and business assets. Our team works closely with financial experts to assess the value of these assets and determine an equitable division that serves the needs of both parties. If you are dealing with complex asset division, we can help you navigate the legal process. Visit our Property Division page for more details.

Adoption and Guardianship

Adopting a child or becoming a legal guardian is a joyful process, but it also comes with legal challenges that require expert guidance. We assist families with all aspects of adoption law, whether you are adopting a child through an agency, through a stepparent adoption, or through private arrangements. We also provide support for those seeking guardianship of a minor or an incapacitated adult.

Our team will guide you through the legal requirements of adoption or guardianship, ensuring that all necessary documentation is filed and that the process is completed efficiently. Whether you are seeking to adopt internationally or domestically, we are here to support you every step of the way. For more information, check out our Adoption Guide.

Domestic Violence and Protective Orders

If you are experiencing domestic violence, it is essential to understand your legal rights and options. Our attorneys assist clients in securing protective orders and restraining orders to ensure safety. We help victims of domestic violence navigate the legal process, which includes obtaining temporary or permanent protection orders, as well as other measures to safeguard their well-being and the well-being of their children.

If you are seeking to protect yourself from domestic violence or if you are accused of such violence, our attorneys are here to provide legal advice and representation. We advocate for our clients’ safety and work to ensure that their rights are upheld. For more details, visit our Domestic Violence page.
